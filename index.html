About
Club

The TJ Cubing Club is a place for people of all experience levels. If you do not know how to solve a Rubik's Cube, we'll have an officer teach you. For those who already solve, we have experienced members who can give insight on how to improve your time. Our club is a place where you can relax and have fun solving cubes.

Team

Each year, our club forms an 8-person team to compete at the annual You Can Do The Rubik's Cube competition. Over the last 5 years, our team has consistently broken the world record, placing 1st, 3 out of 5 times.

In the spring, we will conduct tryouts to determine the speed of each aspiring team member. Our executive board will then select the team members based upon a holistic view, including tryout times, club commitment, and teamwork qualities. Each member is required to commit a couple weeks from their schedule to team practices and meetings.
